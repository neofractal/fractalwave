Melkhal Pulse:

A Symphony Across Time

sound installation/performance

time / SUN 19.02.2023

venue / Melkhal Enschede

We are going to take over the Melkhal factory on 19 February for a spatial and temporal sound dialogue. Whether you are a visitor, a resident, or a visiting alien, you will be enveloped in the day’s industrial pulse, a milky flow of sound.
